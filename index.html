about me

My name is Jordy Rodriguez. I graduated from Harvard College, where I majored in political science (technology science focus) and discovered my passion for solving social problems with people-centered solutions. My academic pursuits trained me to critically evaluated the existing paradigms around technology deployment and how they may test American values and principles.

My professional experiences forged me into a generalist, but I enjoy supporting growing technology businesses develop, refine, and implement their go-to-market operations. My roles at HP, Meta, Altman Solon and Aprende taught me the importance of bridging strategy with the client experience - the space where GTM teams live. I’ve been a member of technical and client-facing teams, which helps me effectively communicate cross-functionally.

I keep up with psychology and demographic trends and am currently learning how human-AI interaction is changing the way we socialize. I’m an active weightlifter, 5K runner and espresso connoisseur.

I always have time to talk about my faith, Real Madrid, F1 and music. I'm a practicing Catholic.
